Cleanup Operations

Regular river and riverbank cleanups remove plastics, debris, and waste. We combine volunteers with local job creation to maintain momentum between major events.

Example: 5,383 bags removed across 17 cleanups in March 2022 (newsletter). Source: Hennops Revival “Riverlution! News”.

Litter Traps & Infrastructure

Where feasible, we operate litter trap systems to intercept floating waste before it moves downstream. Teams are deployed to clear, sort, and remove trapped material safely.

General project approach corroborated by Hennops Revival and Climate360 profile.

Restoration & Nature-based Solutions

  • Soil regeneration and erosion control
  • Phytoremediation support actions
  • Wetland revitalization

Source: Climate360 finalist profile.

Education & Advocacy

We run awareness campaigns, communicate water safety, and share operating tips for community cleanup teams. We also advocate for improved policy and infrastructure maintenance.

Sources: Hennops Revival site (Volunteers category); organization mission.

Impact Over Time

  • 2,000,000 kg removed milestone reported publicly in 2022 (press)
  • 5,000,000+ kg removed (client-provided metric, Feb 2024)

Sources: GoodThingsGuy (2M kg); client metrics.
